About Tanu Harode

A lifetime dedicated to the art of Hindustani Classical Vocal & Soulful Ghazals

The Journey of a Classical Vocalist

{{ site_settings.bio_brief }}

From early childhood, Tanu showed a natural affinity for melody and rhythm. She pursued this passion academically, securing her Master of Arts (MA) in Classical Vocal Music. This rigorous academic grounding, paired with standard traditional Guru-Shishya training, shaped her understanding of Raga aesthetics, voice culture, and the art of voice modulation.

Her signature style is marked by a deep, resonant vocal texture and absolute clarity in pitch (Sur). She specializes in the classical framework of Khayal, as well as light classical forms such as Bhajans. Beyond traditional classical music, her expertise encompasses **Light Music** and **Bollywood Music**. Her customized teaching methodologies cover structured **Voice Culture**, intensive **Riyaz Sessions**, **Karaoke Music** performance training, detailed **Ornamentation Sessions** (techniques like *murki*, *harkat*, and *tana*), and prep work for **Stage Performances** to help students sing with confidence and professional flair.

"Music is not merely sound; it is a conversation between the soul and the divine. A ghazal is the bridge of words and melody that carries this conversation." — Tanu Harode

Distinctions & Education

Akashvani Recognition

All India Radio Graded Artist

Awarded an official artist grading by Akashvani (All India Radio) in Ghazal singing. This prestigious national distinction recognizes consistent vocal purity, artistic integrity, and dedication to the preservation of Indian semi-classical music. Her recitals are periodically broadcasted on regional radio stations.

Academic Credentials

MA in Classical Vocal Music

Completed her postgraduate studies in Hindustani Classical Vocal Music. The program provided rigorous theoretical and practical training in complex Ragas, ancient classical compositions, musicology, and performance dynamics.

Academic Pedagogy

Music Instructor at People's International School

Serves as the lead music coach and director of cultural performing arts at People's International School. In this role, she designs foundational vocal curricula, coaches young talents for national competitions, and directs large-scale choral and theatrical showcases.

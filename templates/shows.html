Technical & Performance Details

To ensure the highest acoustic experience, we provide a complete technical blueprint (Rider) for events. Depending on the venue size and show type, the following details are coordinated:

  • Acoustics: High-grade vocal and instrument microphones (Shure/Sennheiser) and professional sound mixing consoles are required.
  • Accompaniment: Custom combinations of Harmonium, Tabla, Keyboard, Flute, or Acoustic Guitar can be arranged.
  • Travel & Lodging: For performances outside Bhopal, travel expenses (flight/train tickets) and standard double-room lodging for the band are coordinated by the host.

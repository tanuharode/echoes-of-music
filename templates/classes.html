Comprehensive Curriculum

What You Will Learn

A systematic blend of technical voice coaching and lyrical expression suitable for hobbyists, exam candidates, and aspiring performers.

Voice Culture & Breath Control

Learn specialized breathing exercises (Pranayama for singers) to build stamina, sustain long notes, and control voice volume without straining the vocal cords.

Hindustani Classical Ragas

Develop a solid foundation in Ragas (scales), Alankars (patterns), and Swara-patterns. Study the framework of primary thaats and learn to improvise (Aalap & Taan).

Ghazal Rendition & Urdu Diction

Understand the lyrical poetry of Ghazals. Learn correct pronunciation (Talaffuz) of Urdu sounds, and master vocal embellishments like Murkis, Khatkas, and Kan-swaras.

Rhythm (Taal) & Ear Training

Gain control over rhythm cycles (Teentaal, Dadra, Keherwa, Rupak). Improve your musical ear (Kanswar) to stay perfectly in tune with the Tanpura and Harmonium.

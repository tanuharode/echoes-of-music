Meet the Artist

Tanu Harode

Akashvani (All India Radio) Graded Ghazal Singer, MA in Classical Music, and Senior Music Educator.

With decades of dedicated riyaaz (practice) and an academic foundation in Indian Classical Vocal, Tanu Harode brings an unparalleled depth of emotion to every performance and lecture. As the resident music instructor at People's International School, she balances academic mentorship with dynamic live concerts across India.

Whether you want to master the delicate inflections of a ghazal, understand classical raga frameworks, or host an unforgettable evening of live melody, she welcomes you to her musical sanctuary.
